## Why the variant exists

WJF asked for the diesel comparator's trial totals to underpin an EV CO₂-savings
comparison, proposing the **cumulative-counter difference** method (trial-end minus
trial-start). The briefing's page-1 totals implement exactly that (raw basis), and a new
TANK-TO-WHEEL EMISSIONS card carries CO₂e = total fuel × **2.58354 kg CO₂e/L** (the
WJF-agreed tank-to-wheel factor; keep full precision so their arithmetic reproduces).

## Per-vehicle quirks (reusable)

1. **LFC fuel counter RESET on 2026-06-23** (49,520 → 0 L): a naive end-minus-start goes
   negative. `_diesel_raw_kpi_totals` sums positive steps, so the reset is handled; any
   future diesel needs the same care.
2. **Quantised counters**: LFC jumps in 0.5 L steps at ~1 s cadence — the EV-style pure
   `Δ ≤ max_rate × Δt` guard misreads every real jump as a spike (it silently discarded
   ~28,700 L on the first attempt). Hence the `max(QUANTUM, rate×Δt)` step ceiling.
3. **Trunk-haul filter is essential for the mass/temperature analyses**: over all trips
   the FC-vs-GVM fit is noise (0.07 L/100km/t, R² ≈ 0.01) because low-speed urban legs
   run 40–80 L/100km at any mass. At avg speed ≥ 50 km/h: slope 0.55 L/100km/t,
   R² 0.39, and the temperature trend turns physically correct (~1.1 L/100km per 10 °C
   colder). Distribution + speed figures keep all valid trips.
4. **GVM band** (recorded in `briefing_vehicle_specs.json`): bobtail 7–9 t (this tractor
   is lighter than the EV fleet's ~10–12 t bobtails), unladen band [12, 18) median ~17 t,
   laden ≥ 18 t median ~22 t; **`full_laden_t: 40`** overrides the fleet 42 t reference
   (rated GCW 40 t; CVW data tops out at exactly 40.0).
5. **Two 40 t legs carry no fuel value** → they are in the trunk subset but not the fit;
   the dashed full-laden extension must start from the max *fitted* mass (33 t), not
   `nanmax` of all masses, else no dashed tail is drawn (fixed in `build_diesel_charts`).
6. SRF has logger data to 2026-07-06 (~708 km) that is not yet in the local reports —
   the counters will pick it up on the next data-collection-monitor extension.
